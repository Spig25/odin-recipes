Instant Ramen

Ramen Is Easy!

Authentic Japanese ramen can be difficult to make and very time consuming. However, instant ramen requires only minutes to prepare and is dirt cheap.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen Cup
  • High Quality H2O
  • Disregard for Nutrition

Steps

  1. Put water in cup up the fill line
  2. Microwave the cup for about 3 minutes
  3. Grab a fork and get back to studying!
