California Roll

An Easy Sushi Roll

A California roll is an easy to make roll filled with avocado, imitation crab and cucumber. It can be made very quickly and with no need for raw ingredients so it can please those who are picky about raw foods. Dont forget the sushi rice of course!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori Sheets
  • Seasoned Rice Vinegar
    • Vinegar
    • Sugar
    • Salt
  • Avocado
  • Imitation Crab
  • Cucumber
  • Bamboo Sushi Roller

Steps

  1. First we cook the sushi in a rice cooker. You can use a pot if you want to make your life harder but I like my food to be as easy as possible.
  2. Mix in the vinegar into the finished rice. Let cool in the fridge for a bit until it is cool enough to be handled by hand. You can also cool it in the freezer but you just have to be more careful and not leave it in too long.
  3. Cut your Nori sheet in half and place it on the bamboo roller.
  4. Scoop the rice onto the Nori and push the rice gently around to cover the half sheet. Flip the Nori over and place avocado, imitation crab and long sliced cucumber on top.
  5. Proceed to roll the sushi with the bamboo roller while gently squeezing the roll to keep it tight
  6. Cut the roll into eigths. Enjoy!
