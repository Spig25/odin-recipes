PB&J

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Everyone loves PB&J! It is easy to make and delicious. It is a classic food eaten by just about every American at some point.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Start by grabbing two slices of bread
  2. Then we apply jelly to one slice and peanut butter to the other
  3. Put the two slices together and enjoy easy peasy!
